Bienvenue au Bistro Odette

We are a couple-owned bistro with great passion for food and wine in the French kitchen. The menu keeps evolving with the season. The wines are from small producers focusing on the terroir it’s from. Use us as your daily dinner bistro/wine bar or visit us for your anniversary – any reason is a good Bistro Odette reason.

Book a Table

We’re happy to take your booking online. Each reservation is for 2.5 hours – just the right pace for a relaxed meal. You can book up to two months ahead and if we're fully booked, you’re welcome to join the waiting list. We’ll give you a call if something opens up!

For groups larger than 6, please send us an email or give us a call – we’ll do our best to make it work.

Feeling spontaneous? We always keep a few spots open for walk-ins.

Running a bit late? Just call and let us know – we’ll hold your table for a little while.
